Web Developer

Job Description

We're looking for a skilled Front-End Web Developer responsible for designing and implementing responsive, user-friendly interfaces for a growing e-commerce platform, ensuring seamless customer experiences across web and mobile applications.

Salary and Reporting Line

  • Salary: $80,000 – $100,000 per annum (depending on experience)
  • Reporting Line: You will be reporting to the Head of Digital Experience / Lead Software Engineer

Key Role Responsibilities

  1. Develop and maintain responsive, high-performance web interfaces for the e-commerce platform
  2. Collaborate with UX/UI designers to translate design concepts into functional user interfaces
  3. Optimise website performance, speed, and scalability
  4. Ensure cross-browser and cross-device compatibility
  5. Integrate front-end components with back-end services and APIs
  6. Assist in improving online workflows such as checkout, product browsing, and account management
  7. Troubleshoot and resolve user interface issues
  8. Contribute to continuous improvement of development processes and standards

Essential Requirements

  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Experience with modern front-end frameworks (e.g., React, Angular, or Vue)
  • Understanding of responsive and mobile-first design principles
  • Experience integrating with RESTful APIs
  • Strong problem-solving and debugging skills
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment

Preferable Requirements

  • Experience in e-commerce or digital retail environments
  • Familiarity with version control systems (e.g., Git)
  • Knowledge of web accessibility standards (WCAG)
  • Basic understanding of back-end technologies (e.g., Node.js)
  • Experience with performance optimisation and SEO best practices
  • Exposure to Agile or Scrum development methodologies

Junior E-Commerce Support Assistant

Job Description

We're looking for a entry-level role supporting the day-to-day operations of an online retail platform, assisting with product listings, basic website updates, and customer-facing digital processes.

Salary and Reporting Line

  • Salary: $50,000 – $65,000 per annum (depending on experience)
  • Reporting Line: You will be reporting to the E-Commerce Operations Manager

Key Role Responsibilities

  1. Assist in uploading and maintaining product listings, descriptions, and images
  2. Support updates to website content and promotional banners
  3. Monitor product availability and report stock or listing issues
  4. Help test website features and online workflows (e.g., checkout process)
  5. Respond to basic customer enquiries or escalate issues where required
  6. Maintain accurate digital records and data entry tasks
  7. Support the team with administrative and operational duties

Essential Requirements

  • Basic computer literacy and familiarity with web applications
  • Strong attention to detail and organisational skills
  • Good written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to follow instructions and work as part of a team
  • Willingness to learn new systems and tools

Preferable Requirements

  • Experience with online platforms or content management systems (CMS)
  • Basic understanding of e-commerce or online retail environments
  • Familiarity with Microsoft Office or Google Workspace
  • Interest in digital technology or web development
  • Previous part-time or internship experience in retail or administration
